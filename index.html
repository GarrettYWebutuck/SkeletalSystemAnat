The Skeletal System: An Introduction

The Skeletal System is a network of bones, joints, cartilage, and ligaments(Cords of tissue that hold bones together at joints) throughout a body. They give structure to said body and allow the body to have referance points for movement.(So that you dont have a muscle pulling on another muscle for movement).
